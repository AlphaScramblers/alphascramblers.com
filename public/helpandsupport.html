Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We’ve Got Answers.

 Everything you need to know about stream selection, our psychometric test, and your career path — all in one place.
Some General Questions....
Q: What exactly happens in a counselling session? Do you just talk, or is there a specific process you follow?
A: We start by knowing your preferences and then through our well-developed Psychometric Test we find what's best suitable for you on the basis of the psychometric test report.
Q: How is professional academic counselling different from the career advice I can get from my school teachers, relatives, or online videos?
A: Professional career guindance helps you to acknowledge your strengths and weakness through tested and experienced techniques making your career path visible to you.
Q: How does your psychometric test help in stream selection?
A: Our test evaluates your interests, aptitude, personality, and learning style to recommend the best-fit stream. It's designed to give you clear direction based on scientific analysis, not guesswork.
Q: What happens after the completion of the test?
A: You will receive a detailed on-screen report that includes: Your recommended stream(s) Strength and interest mapping Career fitment analysis Subject suggestions Career and exam information related to your stream
Q: Is this test suitable for all Class 10 students?
A: Yes! It is specially designed for Class 10 students of CBSE, ICSE, and State Boards to support academic decision-making.
Q: Is the test online, and can I take it from home?
A: Yes, the test is fully online. You can take it from any device — mobile, tablet, or laptop — at your convenience.
Q: Can parents reach out to you if they have any queries?
A: Absolutely. Parents or students can call us directly if they have any queries related to the report or stream selection.
Q: Is the test free or paid?
A: We offer both free resources. The detailed psychometric test and report fall under our exclusive free plan.
Q: I'm in Class 10 and unsure which stream to choose—Science, Commerce, or Humanities. My parents prefer Science. How do I decide?
A: It's a tough decision to make but here at alphascramblers we are glad to help you out from this difficulty you can contact us or you can directly access all the service through our website.
Q: How can I contact your team?
A: You can reach us via the "Call Now" button on our website. Our team is happy to assist with any questions you may have.
